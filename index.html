History

Went to college immediately after High-School, wandered aimlessly through ASU's curriculum, and left after 2.5years. Discovered a love of vintage motorcycles, fine wines and software development and began to pursue information about both. The puruit lead me to an associates degree in CIT and the fall '15 harvest at Patty Green. While studying C# in my down time I was confronted with my inadequacies in learning Object Oriented Programming so I decided to enroll with Epicodus.

I currently maintain several bikes, both motor and pedal. Some of which are even mine! I own a 1980 Yamaha xs400 Special and a Cannondale SR400 Road bike, but I help some friends out with theirs. I've worked on a 1994 Kawasaki Vulcan, 2006 KLR 650, 2012 Triumph Bonneville 900, and a 1974 Honda CB 250.
